Learn Kubernetes

A curated list of the best websites, courses, interactive labs, and video channels for learning Kubernetes — based on what the community actually uses and recommends.

Official Documentation & Tutorials

Kubernetes Official Tutorials Free Essential

The official Kubernetes docs include browser-based interactive tutorials (powered by Katacoda) covering basics, configurations, stateless & stateful apps, services, and security. Always up-to-date with the latest release. This is where everyone starts.

Kubernetes Tasks (Official) Free

Step-by-step task guides for real-world operations — managing secrets, configuring networking, setting up storage, and more. Invaluable for CKA/CKAD exam prep.

Kubernetes Concepts (Official) Free

Deep-dive reference for every core concept: Pods, Deployments, Services, Namespaces, RBAC, Scheduling, and the control plane architecture. Bookmark this one.

Interactive Labs & Playgrounds

Killercoda Free Interactive Community Favourite

Browser-based Kubernetes playgrounds with guided scenarios — no installation needed. Covers pods, deployments, services, networking, Helm, and troubleshooting. The successor to Katacoda and widely regarded as the best free hands-on platform.

KodeKloud Kubernetes Labs Interactive Top Rated

Hands-on labs and playgrounds aligned with CKA, CKAD, and CKS exam curricula. Run real commands in realistic clusters. KodeKloud is consistently the most-recommended platform on Reddit and DevOps communities for practical Kubernetes learning. (Subscription required for full access.)

Play with Kubernetes (PWK) Free Interactive

Spin up a real multi-node Kubernetes cluster in your browser in seconds. Great for quick experiments and practice without any setup. Sessions last 4 hours.

IBM CloudLabs — Kubernetes Tutorials Free Interactive

Three free browser-based labs covering containers & Kubernetes essentials, scalable web apps, and log analysis & monitoring. No downloads or credit card required.

K8sVisual — Interactive Visual Tutorials Free Interactive

15+ visual, interactive lessons covering pods, services, deployments, StatefulSets, DaemonSets, RBAC, and more. Great for visual learners who want to see how resources relate.

Structured Courses

KodeKloud — Kubernetes for Beginners (CKA/CKAD) Most Recommended Certification

The single most-recommended Kubernetes course across Reddit, DevOps communities, and certification forums. Created by Mumshad Mannambeth, it combines video lectures with integrated hands-on labs. Covers everything from basics to CKA/CKAD exam readiness. Trustpilot rating: 4.5/5 from 460+ reviews.

Coursera — Architecting with Google Kubernetes Engine Certification

Google's official GKE specialization on Coursera. Structured, instructor-led, and includes hands-on labs via Qwiklabs. Ideal if you're targeting GCP or want a university-style course format.

Coursera — IBM: Introduction to Containers, Kubernetes & OpenShift Free to audit

Holistic overview of Docker, Kubernetes, and OpenShift. Good for beginners who want context on the full container ecosystem, not just K8s in isolation.

Linux Foundation — CKA / CKAD / CKS Certifications Certification Industry Standard

The official CNCF certification paths. The CKA exam curriculum doubles as an excellent learning checklist — even if you don't sit the exam, working through its domains ensures comprehensive coverage. KodeKloud and Killer.sh provide exam-simulator environments.

YouTube Channels

TechWorld with Nana Video Most Recommended

Nana's Kubernetes tutorial series is one of the most-watched on YouTube. Clear, well-structured, beginner-friendly. Her "DevOps Bootcamp" playlist covers Docker, Kubernetes, CI/CD, and GitOps. Perfect starting point for visual learners.

KodeKloud Video

Free Kubernetes tutorials and labs on YouTube that complement their paid courses. Great for CKA/CKAD prep with practical demonstrations.

Abhishek Veeramalla Video

Real-world Kubernetes examples with clear explanations. His Kubernetes playlist is highly recommended for understanding practical day-to-day operations and interview preparation.

Kubernetes Community (Official) Video

Official Kubernetes channel with talks, tutorials, KubeCon recordings, and updates directly from the community. Best for staying current with ecosystem developments.

CNCF (Cloud Native Computing Foundation) Video

Live Kubernetes troubleshooting sessions, project updates, and deep-dive talks from KubeCon. Excellent for intermediate-to-advanced learners.

DevOps Directive Video

Scott Lowe's channel covers Kubernetes and cloud-native topics for both beginners and seasoned professionals. Well-produced, concise, and practical.

freeCodeCamp.org Video

Full-length Kubernetes courses (often 8+ hours) that are completely free. Comprehensive, well-structured, and great for deep weekend study sessions.

Roadmaps & Guides

Kubernetes Roadmap (roadmap.sh) Free Community Favourite

Community-maintained visual roadmap laying out every topic from fundamentals through security, observability, and networking. Perfect for knowing what to learn next and avoiding random tutorial hopping.

DevOpsCube — Kubernetes Learning Roadmap Free

Step-by-step guide with a suggested learning order, free resources, and project ideas. Also links to 35+ hands-on Kubernetes tutorials on the same site.

Kubernetes Learning Path (GitHub) Free

Community-maintained GitHub repo with a structured learning path, free resources, cluster deployment guides, and production case studies. Great companion reference.

CNCF — Top 28 Kubernetes Resources for 2026 Free

Official CNCF-curated list covering docs, interactive labs, roadmaps, podcasts, and community resources. A comprehensive meta-resource that's kept up to date.

Blogs & Written Tutorials

DevOpsCube Kubernetes Tutorials Free

35+ comprehensive hands-on tutorials covering architecture, cluster setup, deployments, best practices, package management, secrets, monitoring, and logging. One of the best free written tutorial collections.

Learn Cloud Native — Beginner's Guide to Kubernetes Free

A 45-page illustrated guide explaining pods, replica sets, services, and core concepts. Originally recommended on r/devops and still one of the clearest written introductions.

Podcasts

Kubernetes Podcast from Google Free

Long-running weekly show with guest insights from the Kubernetes community, news, and commentary on recent releases. Great for staying current during commutes.

Day Two DevOps (Packet Pushers) Free

Conversations with technical leaders on Kubernetes operations, security, networking, and day-2 concerns. Focuses on real-world production experience.
